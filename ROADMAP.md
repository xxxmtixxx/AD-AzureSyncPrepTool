### Update User UPN Suffixes

- **Expected Completion: Q1 2024**
- **Description:** We plan to add a function to the `AD-AzureSyncPrepTool-Import.ps1` script that will update each user's UPN (User Principal Name) Suffix to match the domain of their primary email address. This will help ensure consistency between the user's email address and their UPN.
